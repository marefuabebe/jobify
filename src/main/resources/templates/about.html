Our History

It Started With a Simple Idea

Founded in 2024, Jobify was born from a frustration with the traditional hiring process. We realized that geography shouldn't dictate career potential.

Today, we've grown into a dynamic ecosystem where millions of interactions happen daily. From a small garage startup to a global platform, our mission remains unchanged: to empower professionals to work on their own terms.
